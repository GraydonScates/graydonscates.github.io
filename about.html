About Me

As you may have guessed by now, my name is Graydon Scates and I am both a programmer and a retro gaming fan. I started my coding journey with RPG Maker 2000 and simple HTML pages during middle school and never looked back. After moving from Atlanta to Orlando I attended Full Sail University and graduated Salutatorian with a Bachelor's degree in Digital Art and Design, which was a fun degree to be in as one of the only programming focused people surrounded by more creative artistic types. However, the creative skills I picked up from my classmates ultimately helped to get me where I am today, as did my ability to self-teach any programming language I come across.

For the last 10+ years I have been working for The Walt Disney Company in their in-house Marketing Creative Agency, Yellow Shoes. I started out working on a range of marketing projects like emails and web banners before graduating to micro-sites and games built in Flash (RIP) and Javascript. Eventually, as I continued to grow my knowledge and skills, my duties shifted to more Software Engineering and System Administration tasks out of necessity as I was one of the few people on our small team capable of tackling these more complicated programming needs. Through all of that I developed a deep love of solving complicated application building challenges and creating software solutions used company-wide to help make the lives and jobs of my fellow Disney Cast Members easier.

My newest adventure, aside from a constant string of personal video game projects, is teaching the MERN full stack (Mongo, Express, React & Node) as an instructor for the University of Central Florida Continuing Education Coding Bootcamp. I have taught over a hundred people to be full stack application developers so far and it has been one of the most fun and rewarding challenges I have ever taken on. At this point I don't ever see myself not teaching in some form, as long as my day job continues to afford me the time to do so.

If you ever want to talk about coding, games or even D&D, just check out my contact page!
